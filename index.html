What's In the Reign

Built to be raced, not just played

Every system below exists because a real Death% run turned into a click-speed contest. It doesn't anymore.

01 — FAIRNESS

Seeded Races

Every roll in the game — event order, combat, drops — runs off one seed. Share a seed and race someone on the exact same board. No more losing to bad luck instead of a bad plan.

02 — STAKES

Choices That Can Cost You

A handful of the realm's most tempting offers are gambles now. The reward is real. So is the chance it backfires — a companion's trust gone for good, an army gutted, a kingdom's faith in you cracked open.

03 — COMMITMENT

The All-In Charge

March with half your army, or throw in nearly all of it for a much harder hit. Win, and it's the fastest conquest in the run. Lose, and there may not be an army left to march home.
